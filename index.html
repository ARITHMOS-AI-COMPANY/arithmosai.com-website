What we do

Manufacturing environments produce massive, heterogeneous sensor data across tools, processes, and sites—often noisy, high-dimensional, and hard to standardize. Arithmos AI builds modern architectures that transform raw streams into structured intelligence for monitoring and decision support.

Heterogeneous sensor fusion
Learn jointly from multiple modalities and time scales to capture system behavior under real operating conditions.
Foundation-model architectures
Reusable backbones for industrial time-series data, with task-specific heads where needed.
LLM-compatible workflows
Outputs that support analysis, summarization, and operational reasoning pipelines without exposing internal IP.

Applications

Designed to plug into existing data pipelines and deliver measurable outcomes for pilot partners.

Tool health monitoring
Detect early signals of degradation and reliability risk using multi-sensor context.
Drift & anomaly investigation
Identify distribution shifts and anomalous episodes across time and operating regimes.
Virtual metrology
Infer difficult-to-measure quality or process metrics from sensor data in near real-time.
Cross-tool / cross-site analysis
Compare performance patterns across tools, chambers, or sites with consistent analytics.
